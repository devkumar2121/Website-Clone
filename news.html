Clarification regarding false reports concerning Brady House operations

Contrary to false reports, US Bank has no plans to close operations in the Brady House branch in Mumbai. The Brady House branch continues to operate as normal and remains fully operational. Reallocation of some of the large value accounts is part of the regular restructuring process at US Bank to strengthen internal systems and processes and centralize certain critical functions. Retail operations for US Bank customers continue to operate from the branch.
